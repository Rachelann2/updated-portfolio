PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY

I am a driven UC Davis college graduate, with a degree in Biological Sciences and the human body. I worked all throughout college determined to work hard to support myself while getting a degree. I am energetic, articulate, positive, upbeat and creative. From High School I have always been involved, working as a team with many adults and fellow classmates. My first job was a YMCA Camp Counselor over the summer, working with kids from 5-12 years old. The following year I worked at a San Diego Florist shop arranging flowers and coordinating bouquets. Throughout my four years of studying at UC Davis I have worked at a local gym called TEAMride as a Studio Crew Member in Sacramento, CA. My position consisted of setting up new members on their bikes, helping costumers with memberships and creating an atmosphere for a challenging and enjoyable workout. Finding a passion in the fitness community I took courses through “Spinning” to become a certified spin instructor and have taught rhythm-based spin classes at Fithouse in Davis, CA and was a seasonal hire Educator at Lululemon at Arden Fair mall. At Lululemon I learned about the importance of connection with people and hustle behind hard work. I now currently work at Bueno Sport and Swim as their sales manager as well as teaching climb and cycle at Edge Studios in Sacramento, CA. I have grown as a spin instructor by the demand of music, sweat and motivation I have come to flourish in the atmosphere of leading people through a workout.
